Handmade crochet art

Welcome to Gigis Yarn, a digital gallery for my handmade crochet art.

I started my crochet journey back in 2019 when I made small projects like keychains and small wallets for friends and family. I then started to write my own patterns and make baby accessories and toys.

Today I make customized items and I only use 100% cotton yarn. Have a look in my gallery! You can purchase a finished product or a pattern if you would like to do it yourself.
