Welcome to Gigis Yarn, a digital gallery for my crochet art


I started my crochet journey back in 2019 when I made small projects like keychains and small wallets for friends and family. One day I scrolled through Instagram and I found a post of someones pram mobile with little crochet animals on and I thought - I want to do that! So I then started to experiment and I made animal after animal, looked at youtube videos and tried to find more inspiration so I could write my own patterns.

This site is for inspirational purposes as well as a digital gallery for my little artworks. You can puchase a finished products of your choice or make it yourself with my step-by-step patterns. Have a look in my gallery and fel free to contact me!
