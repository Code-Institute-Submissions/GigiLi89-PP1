This is Gigis Yarn


My name is Gigi and I am a crochet artist, welcome to my digital gallery. I started my crochet journey in 2019 where I started making small wallets and keychains.
One day I saw an Instagram post where someone has made a baby mobile and I thought - I want to do that! Today I make baby accessories and toys and I make them per request. Please contact me if you're interested!
